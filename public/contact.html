Contact NexusCompress Support

Need help with compression settings, UAE portal presets, or a bug report? We’re here — by email.

Email us

NexusCompress is operated by Fun Adventure Media Studio. We typically reply within 1–2 business days (UAE time, Sun–Thu).

Email support

Or write directly: info@funadventure.ae

Before you email

  • Your files stay on your device. We cannot access images you compress — include a screenshot if something looks wrong.
  • Check the FAQ on the homepage for JPEG/WebP settings and privacy questions.
  • See documentation for presets, batch ZIP, and CLI usage.
  • UAE portal sizing? Read our UAE portal format guide.

What to include

  • Browser and device (e.g. Chrome on iPhone, Safari on Mac)
  • Which tool tab (compressor, HEIC, PDF, etc.)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots of any error message
