NexusCompress Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

NexusCompress (“we”, “our”, “the tool”) is a free media toolkit at https://compress.funadventure.ae/, operated by Fun Adventure Media Studio. This policy explains how information is handled when you use NexusCompress.

1. Image processing — no uploads

Your images are not uploaded to our servers for compression. Resizing and compression run entirely in your web browser using JavaScript, Web Workers, and OffscreenCanvas. Files remain on your device unless you choose to download the results.

2. Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences (for example theme choice) in local storage
  • Measure traffic and how the tool is used
  • Deliver and personalize advertising

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features.

3. Third-party vendors — advertising (Google AdSense)

We use third-party vendors, including Google, to serve advertisements when you visit NexusCompress.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this website.
  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate usage (pages visited, general location, device type). Analytics data is processed by Google according to the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Local storage

We may store compression settings and recent activity metadata in your browser’s local storage so NexusCompress remembers your preferences. This data does not leave your device except as described above for analytics and advertising.

6. Service worker (offline caching)

We may cache parts of the application in your browser for faster loading. No personal images are stored in the cache.

7. Children’s privacy

NexusCompress is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: info@funadventure.ae (Fun Adventure Media Studio, operator of NexusCompress).
