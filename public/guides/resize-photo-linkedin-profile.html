Profile photo: crop to square

  1. Open NexusCompress.
  2. Under Aspect ratio, select 1:1 (square).
  3. Set max width to 800 px, format JPEG, quality 85%.
  4. Drop your photo, download, upload to LinkedIn.

Tip: Upload at 800×800 px — LinkedIn displays smaller but starts with more detail for a sharper result.

Banner image

Crop to 1584 × 396 px (4:1). Set max width 1584 px before upload.

Post images

Use the Social post — JPEG, 1080px preset or Custom at 1200 px max width, 85% quality. Keep under 1 MB for fast feed loading.

Frequently asked questions

What size should a LinkedIn profile photo be?

Upload a square photo at 800×800 px in JPEG format.

What are the LinkedIn banner dimensions?

1584×396 px (4:1 ratio) in JPEG or PNG.

Why does my profile photo look blurry?

LinkedIn recompresses uploads. Start with a sharp 800×800 px image — avoid already-heavily-compressed sources.

JPEG or PNG for LinkedIn?

JPEG for photos and banners. PNG for logos with sharp edges if needed.
