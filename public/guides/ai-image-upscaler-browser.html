NexusCompress · AI image upscaler FAQ

Enlarge photos 2× or 4× with Real-ESRGAN — not stretched pixels. The AI Upscaler runs entirely in your browser; images are never uploaded to a server.

AI upscale vs normal resize

Bicubic or Lanczos resizing only interpolates between existing pixels, which often looks soft or blurry when enlarged. Real-ESRGAN is trained on image pairs to reconstruct plausible detail — textures, edges, and fine structure — on your device via ONNX Runtime Web.

Best use cases

  • Old low-resolution family photos
  • Product images from suppliers
  • AI-generated art before printing
  • Scanned documents before portal upload

Privacy & offline use

The model file (~5 MB) downloads once and is cached locally. After that, upscaling can run without an internet connection. Inference runs in a Web Worker so the page stays responsive.

How to upscale

  1. Open AI Upscaler.
  2. Upload JPEG, PNG, or WebP.
  3. Choose 2× or 4× and export JPEG or PNG.
  4. Use the before/after slider to compare results.

Frequently asked questions

How is AI upscaling different from regular resizing?

Standard resizing interpolates pixels; Real-ESRGAN reconstructs detail with a neural network — in your browser on NexusCompress.

What is the best use case for AI upscaling?

Photos, portraits, product shots, old scans, and AI-generated images you need at print resolution.

Does the upscaler work offline?

Yes, after the model is cached on first use. No further uploads are required.
