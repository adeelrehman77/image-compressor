NexusCompress · Compress photos for UAE government portals

How to shrink Emirates ID, visa, and MOHRE scans to 200 KB or 500 KB — without uploading sensitive documents to the internet.

Why 200 KB and 500 KB limits?

UAE smart-service portals (ICA, MOHRE, RTA, ICP) often reject oversized uploads. A phone scan of a passport or Emirates ID can easily exceed 2–5 MB — you need smart compression that keeps text readable.

Recommended NexusCompress settings

  • Emirates ID / ICA: 200 KB, JPEG, ~80% quality
  • MOHRE / RTA: 500 KB, JPEG or WebP
  • Enable target file size — the tool automatically lowers quality until the file fits

Select UAE Portal Presets in the sidebar on NexusCompress — everything runs locally in your browser.
