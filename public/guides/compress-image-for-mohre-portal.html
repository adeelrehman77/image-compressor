NexusCompress · Compress image for MOHRE / labour portal

MOHRE is used for work permits, labour contracts, and visa processing. Like most UAE government portals, it enforces strict file size limits that catch people off guard.

MOHRE portal upload requirements

Document typeFormatMax size
Passport copyJPEG500 KB
Emirates ID scanJPEG500 KB
Visa copyJPEG500 KB
Worker photoJPEG200 KB
Employment contract (scan)JPEG / PDF500 KB
Educational certificatesJPEG500 KB

Note: MOHRE requirements can change. Check the portal's current upload instructions before submitting.

Step-by-step: compress a document for MOHRE

  1. Open NexusCompress — documents stay on your device.
  2. Under UAE Portal Presets, select MOHRE / labour portal — 500 KB JPEG.
  3. Drop your scanned document or photo.
  4. The tool sets JPEG format and lowers quality until the file is under 500 KB.
  5. Download and upload directly to the portal.

Batch processing multiple worker documents

Drop all files at once, apply the MOHRE preset, then download everything as a ZIP. Each file is individually compressed under 500 KB.

Tip: Flatbed scan at 150 DPI typically produces a JPEG well under 500 KB without extra compression.

What if my document is a PDF?

Use PDF Merge & Split to extract pages, or Images to PDF if you need to combine scans — then compress each JPEG under 500 KB.

Worker passport photo requirements

Worker photos must be under 200 KB JPEG with a white background. Use Passport Studio to crop and resize before compressing.

Frequently asked questions

What is the maximum file size for MOHRE portal uploads?

Most MOHRE document uploads have a 500 KB limit in JPEG format. Worker photos are limited to 200 KB.

What documents need to be uploaded to MOHRE?

Common uploads include passport copies, visa copies, Emirates ID scans, worker photos, employment contracts, and educational certificates.

Can I compress multiple MOHRE documents at once?

Yes. Drop all files, apply the MOHRE preset, and download a ZIP with all compressed files.

Is it safe to compress passport and ID scans online?

With NexusCompress, all processing happens in your browser. Scans never leave your device.
