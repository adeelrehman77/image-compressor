NexusCompress · Resize photo for UAE visa application

ICA visa and entry permit applications require a passport-size photo in specific dimensions and file size. Getting this wrong is one of the most common reasons uploads fail.

UAE visa photo requirements

RequirementValue
Dimensions35 × 45 mm (passport size)
File formatJPEG only
Max file size200 KB
BackgroundWhite or off-white
Face coverage70–80% of frame (chin to crown)
ExpressionNeutral, eyes open, mouth closed
Digital resolution600 × 800 px minimum

Common mistake: Uploading a smartphone portrait (3–5 MB) without resizing causes the upload to fail or the photo to be rejected.

How to resize and compress your visa photo

  1. Open Passport Studio on NexusCompress.
  2. Select Emirates ID & Visa (35×45 mm) under UAE Presets.
  3. Upload your portrait — it stays on your device.
  4. Drag the crop ring so your face fills 70–80% of the circle (hairline to chin).
  5. Click Download Digital Photo — output is JPEG under 200 KB.

Tip: Click Generate 4×6 Print Sheet for a lab-ready 300 DPI layout at UAE print shops and pharmacies.

Already cropped — just over 200 KB?

Use the main image compressor. Under UAE Portal Presets, select Emirates ID / passport copy — 200 KB JPEG. The tool reduces quality until the file is under 200 KB.

White background required

ICA requires a white or off-white plain background. Retake in front of a white wall if needed — Passport Studio preserves your original background.

Frequently asked questions

What size photo is required for a UAE visa application?

ICA requires a 35×45 mm photo, JPEG under 200 KB with a white background.

Can I use a phone photo for UAE visa upload?

Yes — crop to 35×45 mm and compress under 200 KB as JPEG using Passport Studio.

Is a scanned photo accepted?

Yes, if it meets dimension, format, and size requirements. Scan at 150 DPI minimum, save as JPEG, compress under 200 KB.

How do I compress without losing face detail?

Use JPEG quality between 75–85%. Facial detail is preserved and the file stays under 200 KB for most photos.
