NexusCompress · Compress images for real estate listings

Agents and landlords upload dozens of property photos to Bayut, Property Finder, and Dubizzle. Pre-compressing speeds uploads and gives you sharper results after portal recompression.

UAE real estate portal guidelines

PortalFormatMax per fileMax photos
BayutJPEG5 MBUp to 30
Property FinderJPEG5 MBUp to 30
DubizzleJPEG5 MBUp to 20
HouzaJPEG5 MBUp to 25

Pro tip: Upload at 1–1.5 MB per photo even though portals allow 5 MB. Portals re-compress anyway — a well-optimised source looks better than a 7 MB RAW export.

Recommended settings

  • Format: JPEG (portals do not accept WebP)
  • Quality: 85%
  • Max width: 1920 px
  • Target size: 500 KB – 1.5 MB per photo

Batch compress a property shoot

  1. Open NexusCompress.
  2. Use Custom: JPEG, 85% quality, 1920 px max width — or Website preset then switch output to JPEG.
  3. Click Choose folder and select your shoot folder.
  4. Download as ZIP when done.
  5. Upload compressed files to the portal.

Watermarking listing photos

Use the Watermark (batch) option to add your agency logo before download. Set position (bottom-right is standard) and opacity.

JPEG vs WebP for portals

Use JPEG for Bayut, Dubizzle, and Property Finder. WebP is only relevant for your own website or CMS.

Frequently asked questions

What size is best for Bayut and Property Finder?

JPEG at 1920 px wide, 85% quality, under 1.5 MB per photo.

How do I watermark all photos at once?

Use Watermark (batch) in NexusCompress — upload logo, set position, compress, download ZIP.

Can I compress 30+ photos at once?

Yes. Large batches process one file at a time to keep your browser stable, then download as ZIP.

Does Dubizzle accept WebP?

No. Use JPEG for all UAE property portal uploads.
