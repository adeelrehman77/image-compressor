NexusCompress · Resize and compress images for Instagram

Instagram re-compresses uploads. Starting with the right dimensions and a smaller file helps your posts stay sharp.

Recommended dimensions (2025)

  • Feed square — 1080 × 1080 px
  • Feed portrait — 1080 × 1350 px
  • Feed landscape — 1080 × 566 px
  • Stories / Reels — 1080 × 1920 px

Compression tips

Use the Social preset in NexusCompress (JPEG, 1080px max width, 80% quality) or set max width to 1080 and format to JPEG for maximum compatibility when posting from mobile.

For carousel graphics with text, PNG may be sharper; for photos, JPEG or WebP is usually better.

Privacy

Unlike cloud tools, NexusCompress processes photos locally — useful for unpublished campaign assets and client content.
